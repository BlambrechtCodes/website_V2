About Us

What is CODERS?

CODERS is a community of UWL students scattered across a variety of departments who share common interests of coding, technology, and computer science.

Our Mission

CODERS is committed to enriching the La Crosse community by volunteering, teaching fundamental computer science skills, supporting student development and career readiness, and fostering diversity and inclusion in technology.
