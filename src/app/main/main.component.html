Welcome to CODERS!

What is the club?

CODERS is a club dedicated to enhancing the La Crosse community by promoting computer science education, supporting students, and fostering diversity in tech.

We also have free cookies!!!

What do we do?

  • Community outreach: We teach fundamental computer science skills to local residents, empowering them with technological knowledge for the betterment of the entire community.
  • Student support: We provide comprehensive support to students on campus, promoting their professional skills to enhance academic performance and career readiness.
  • Diversity promotion: We actively promote diversity in computer science by encouraging participation from all minority groups and serving as a support network for all students, fostering an inclusive environment where everyone can thrive.
